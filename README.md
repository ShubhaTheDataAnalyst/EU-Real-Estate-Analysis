# 🏡 European Real Estate Analysis – Power BI Project

**This project analyzes a European real estate dataset containing 5,000+ property listings.  
The goal is to uncover meaningful insights about pricing, rent, amenities, property types, and market trends.
---**

## 📊 Dataset Overview
The dataset includes:
- Property price  
- Monthly rent  
- Area (sq.m)  
- Bedrooms & bathrooms  
- Energy rating  
- Floor number  
- Amenities (gym, parking, pool, security, etc.)  
- Furnishing type  
- City  
- Construction year  
- Listing date  

Total rows: **5000**  
Total columns: **25**

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### **1. City-Level Price Differences**
Paris stands out as a premium real estate market compared to Brussels.
Paris has more than double the listings, reflecting higher market activity, and its properties are priced at a significant premium — with an average price of €1.01M and €5.88K per sqm, almost twice Brussels’ €581.7K average price and €2.99K per sqm. This highlights Paris as a high-value, high-demand market, while Brussels offers a more affordable and value-efficient alternative for buyers and investors.

### **2. Area vs Price**
Even though apartments make up most of the market (41.14%), they remain relatively affordable with an average price of €363.62K. Warehouses, on the other hand, are rare (4.92%) but extremely expensive, averaging €1.06M. This shows that warehouses are a high-value niche asset, while apartments drive the everyday housing market.

### **3. Floor Number Matters**
Higher floors (10–19) tend to have higher prices, likely due to better views and less noise.
Average price increases with floor numbers.

### **4. Energy Efficiency Impacts Value**
Homes with energy ratings A–C have noticeably higher prices and rent.

### **5. Furnished Properties Earn More Rent**
Fully furnished and semi-furnished properties are listing for sale over unfurnished ones.

### **6. Most Popular Property Types**
2BHK and 3BHK properties dominate listings and demand.

### **7. Amenities Increase Value**
Properties with amenities like a gym, pool, or parking show higher sale and rental values.

### **8. Newer Buildings Sell for More**
Construction year after 2020 correlates with a strong price premium.

### **9. Listing Activity Increasing**
More properties were listed after 2022, showing growing market activity.
